Current Events
  • Our 4H club is hosting a Dog-A-Thon on July 20th. We are going to have a scavengers hunt around Blowing Rock with our dogs. You will eventually end up at Moses Cone. We are partnering up with the Watauga Humane Society for this event. You can take one of their dogs on the hike and if you are interested in the dog you could adopt it afterward! We will also be providing water for you and your dogs on the hike, and we’ll have a cook out at the end. We look foward to seeing you there.
